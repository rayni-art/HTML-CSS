Who We Are



Founder Rayni is a make up artist and entrepreneur.
After many years she work in this field, she recognize the
need and demand for natural and organic personal care products.
Her goal is to reduce consumer exposure to industrial, agricultural,
and cosmetic chemicals.She also sees that cosmetic packaging is
harming the environment because most of the cosmetic packaging
is made using plastic.With these things in mind, she started
making households a safe and harmful chemical-free environment for
families in 2019.
